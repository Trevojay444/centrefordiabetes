About Us

Best Healthcare

The Centre for Diabetes Management (CDM) is a privately owned medical centre in Harare founded by Physician/Diabetologist Specialist Dr. Mangwiro, , CDM is an accredited leader in diabetes management and its related complications. Home to at least seven specialists, the Centre offers a wide range of services.

Exceptional Care

Always striving to meet your health care needs and provide the best experience possible, Dr Magwiro is passionate about providing personalised, comprehensive, and patient-centred care of the very highest quality. Your wellbeing is Dr Magwiro’s priority.

Genuine Care

The Centre for Diabetes Management Hospital’s personalised approach to care planning, treatment and follow-up support is second to none. You can expect professionalism, compassion, and patient-centred care in your dealings with us.
